Design Comp V2

Maya Mekonnen

I adjusted the background color slightly and chose a new heading font, Bebas Neue. I think this font is more impactful and powerful, leading towards the idea of protest. Also, instead of the line leading to the description I decided to have the descriptions pop up in the same place as the user hovers over various protests. This way, they are not always looking for where the pop up is as they change positions every time. I included the menu which includes some related and useful links. I am not 100% sure about the styling of the menu, but I will continue to play around with it.

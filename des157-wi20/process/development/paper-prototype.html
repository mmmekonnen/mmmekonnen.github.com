Maya Mekonnen

I want my project to highlight the power of protest and showcase successful examples of protest across the United States. The map will be covered with dots, indicating the place of protest. The user will be able to zoom and move around the map.

When the user hovers over a dot on the map, there will be a pop up window that provides the name of the protest and a short summary of the event. In the pop up bubble will be a "see more" or "learn more" button that the user can click to learn more about the event and its effects.

Once a user clicks to learn more about a protest, they are brough to a page with a automatic slide show of images of the protest as well as a more detail description of the occasion and its impact. The user can also view similar or related protests that they might be interested in learning about.

In the menu, there will be links to pages like the history and growth of protest, protest near you and a link that will provide the user with contact information in the case of questions or requests.

User Research

Maya Mekonnen

This process allowed me to layout the real wants and needs of my potential user. I recognized that besides people wanting to attend protests, there will also be users who want to post the protests that they are hosting. This persona-making process brought to light the importance of having a sharing feature because protests are nothing without the attendance by the masses. Also, there are many criteria that people may have for protests that they want to be a part of. My site should allow people to find exactly the kinds of opportunities that they want because no one wants to attend protests for causes that they are not passionate about.
